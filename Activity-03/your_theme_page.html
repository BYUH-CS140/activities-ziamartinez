Tokyo, Japan

Top Itinerary Stops

  1. Tsukiji Market

    2. Tsukiji Outer Market is and will continue to be the best marketplace for customers to buy fresh seafood, vegetables, and Japanese traditional food materials. 1

  2. Imperial Palace

    3. The Imperial Palace has served as the residential place of the successive Emperors since 1868.2

  3. Skytree Tower

    4. The Tokyo Skytree (東京スカイツリー) is a television broadcasting tower and landmark of Tokyo.3

  4. Senso-ji Temple

    5. Senso-ji (浅草寺) is Tokyo's oldest temple, completed in 645 AD. It is located in Asakusa, Tokyo.4

  5. Japan Tokyo Temple

    6. The Tokyo Japan Temple is the 100th operating temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church).5

  6. Tokyo Tower

    7. Tokyo Tower (東京タワー) is a communications and observation tower in the Shiba-koen district of Minato, Tokyo, Japan.6

Items to Pack

  • Passport and Visa
  • Comfortable Walking Shoes
  • Camera
  • Train Map
  • Cash or Japanese Card
