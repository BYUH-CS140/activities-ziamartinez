Seoul, South Korea

Top Itinerary Stops

  1. Gyeongbokgung Palace

    2. The main royal palace of the Joseon dynasty, a must-see historical site with traditional architecture.1

  2. N Seoul Tower

    3. An iconic tower with panoramic views of the city, popular for love locks and nighttime lights.2

  3. Myeongdong Shopping Street

    4. A vibrant shopping district known for K-beauty, fashion, and street food.2

  4. Bukchon Hanok Village

    5. This village preserves traditional Korean houses and offers a glimpse into the past.3

  5. COEX Mall & Starfield Library

    6. One of Asia’s largest underground malls, featuring a stunning public library.4

  6. Cheonggyecheon Stream

    7. A peaceful urban stream that runs through downtown Seoul, perfect for evening strolls.2

Items to Pack

  • Passport and K-ETA
  • Korean Transit Card (T-Money)
  • Comfortable Shoes
  • Power Adapter (220V)
  • Cash and Credit Card
