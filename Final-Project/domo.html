Domo Projects

Real-time Business Management and Data Automation

Domo’s business management platform is designed to turn disparate data into actionable insights, providing the context necessary for faster, better-informed decisions. While traditional reporting often leads to backlogs, Domo automates these processes by connecting to your data in real time, empowering dynamic business intelligence. Explore how I've leveraged Domo to create powerful, automated reporting solutions.

BYU–Hawaii: Graduation Rate Performance Analysis

Challenge: BYU–Hawaii needed a clear and consistent way to compare its graduation performance against regional and national peer institutions, track completion rates from 4-year to 8-year, and monitor longitudinal data trends alongside undergraduate headcount.

My Role & Solution: I developed an interactive Domo dashboard focused on institutional graduation performance. This involved:

  • Integrating various data sources including student records and institutional benchmarks.
  • Building calculated metrics to track 4-year, 6-year, and 8-year graduation rates.
  • Designing comparative visualizations to benchmark BYU–Hawaii against other institutions.
  • Implementing trend lines and historical data views for long-term strategic planning.

Impact: The dashboard provided university leadership and academic departments with critical insights into student success, enabling them to identify areas for improvement in student retention and program efficacy, ultimately supporting strategic initiatives to enhance graduation rates.

BYU–Hawaii: Student Academic Success Summary

Challenge: The university required a comprehensive snapshot of key academic success indicators, including student headcount, graduation and retention rates, financial aid distribution, and demographic trends (specifically by ethnicity) over a multi-year period (2017 to 2022).

My Role & Solution: I designed and implemented a dynamic Domo dashboard summarizing critical student academic success data. My process included:

  • Connecting to and transforming diverse student data sets for unified analysis.
  • Creating detailed visualizations for demographic breakdowns, including ethnic groups.
  • Developing interactive charts to track financial aid distribution and its correlation with retention.
  • Ensuring data refreshes were automated for real-time insights into student progress.

Impact: This dashboard provided academic advisors, institutional research, and administration with a powerful tool to monitor student demographics and performance, informing targeted support programs, resource allocation, and initiatives aimed at improving student outcomes and equity across different groups.
