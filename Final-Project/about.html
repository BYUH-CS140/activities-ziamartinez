Get To Know Me

Bringing Your Data to Life, One Insight at a Time.

My Data Journey: From Spreadsheets to Strategic Insights

My fascination with data began not in a classroom, but in the bustling markets of the Philippines, where I witnessed firsthand the challenges small businesses faced in navigating economic fluctuations and market trends. I saw the power hidden in everyday numbers – sales figures, inventory counts, local statistics – and how understanding them could truly transform operations. My early experience, supporting my family's real estate business with simple spreadsheets to track performance and inform decisions, ignited a lifelong passion for transforming raw data into actionable intelligence.

Moving to Hawaii and pursuing my degree at BYU–Hawaii deepened this commitment. Here, I immersed myself in the technical foundations of data science, honing skills in SQL, Python, R, and advanced analytics tools like Domo, Power BI, and Tableau. My academic journey, enriched by projects in areas like predictive modeling and data visualization, allowed me to see data not merely as information, but as a universal language for understanding and driving change. I learned to extract meaningful narratives from complex datasets, ensuring insights are not only accurate but also ethically sound and impactful.

My Vision: Empowering Through Data

I believe that data, when harnessed thoughtfully, has the power to inspire and empower. My vision is to help individuals and organizations leverage data-driven insights to spark meaningful impact and foster sustainable innovation, especially in contexts where data literacy might be evolving.

My Mission: Transforming Complexity into Clarity

My mission is to transform complex data into clear, actionable insights that support smarter decisions, amplify stories worth telling, and create opportunities for growth. I am driven by the desire to make sophisticated analytics accessible and meaningful, bridging the gap between raw information and strategic action, particularly for those who may have historically been underserved by technological advancements.

My Core Values: The Pillars of My Practice

  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical rigor in every dataset analyzed, every model built, and every recommendation made.
  • Curiosity: Continuously exploring new methodologies, algorithms, and industry trends to push the boundaries of what data can reveal.
  • Empathy: Designing human-centered data solutions that are intuitive, accessible, and truly resonate with the diverse needs of users.
  • Collaboration: Fostering strong partnerships and believing that the most impactful insights emerge from diverse perspectives working in concert.
  • Excellence: Committing to the highest standards of analytical precision, innovative problem-solving, and continuous professional growth.
