Tableau Projects

Creating Visual Stories from Complex Data

Tableau is a leading Business Intelligence (BI) and data visualization tool, renowned for its ability to transform raw data into interactive, insightful dashboards. Its drag-and-drop interface and powerful analytical capabilities make data exploration intuitive for users across various skill levels. Below, you'll find examples of how I leverage Tableau to uncover patterns, track key metrics, and enable data-driven strategies.

iFlix: Global Content Analytics Dashboard

Challenge: A global streaming platform, iFlix, needed a comprehensive dashboard to analyze its vast content library, understand distribution trends, and identify popular content attributes across different regions and genres.

My Role & Solution: I developed an interactive Tableau dashboard providing global streaming insights. Key aspects of this project included:

  • Connecting to and preparing a large dataset of content metadata (movies, TV shows).
  • Designing dynamic filters for country, rating, genre, and release year to allow granular exploration.
  • Creating visually engaging charts to represent content distribution, trends over time, and audience preferences.
  • Utilizing Tableau's advanced features to ensure smooth interactivity and performance.

Impact: This dashboard offered content strategists and marketing teams a clear view of global content performance, enabling them to make informed decisions on content acquisition, regional targeting, and genre diversification.

Human Resources: Employee Insights & Attrition Analysis

Challenge: An organization sought a deeper understanding of its workforce to identify factors contributing to attrition, analyze employee demographics, and monitor job satisfaction levels across departments.

My Role & Solution: I created a comprehensive HR Analytics dashboard in Tableau, designed to provide strategic employee insights. My process involved:

  • Consolidating various HR data points (demographics, performance, survey results).
  • Developing calculated fields to track attrition rates, job role distribution, and tenure.
  • Building interactive visualizations for age group and gender breakdowns, departmental insights, and satisfaction scores.
  • Ensuring data security and privacy compliance within the dashboard design.

Impact: This dashboard provided HR managers and leadership with critical insights into workforce dynamics, enabling proactive strategies for talent retention, diversity & inclusion initiatives, and targeted employee engagement programs.
