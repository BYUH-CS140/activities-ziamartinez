Manila, the capital city of the Philippines, is a vibrant metropolis rich in history and culture. Known for its colonial architecture and lively street scenes, Manila offers a blend of the old and new. Visitors can explore historic sites like Intramuros or enjoy modern shopping and dining experiences.
