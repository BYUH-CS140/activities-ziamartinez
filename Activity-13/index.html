Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is a vibrant city that blends the ultramodern with the traditional. From neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples, Tokyo offers a unique travel experience.

Top Itinerary Stops

  1. Tsukiji Market
  2. Imperial Palace
  3. Skytree Tower
  4. Senso-ji Temple
  5. Japan Tokyo Temple
  6. Tokyo Tower

Tsukiji Outer Market is and will continue to be the best marketplace for customers to buy fresh seafood, vegetables, and Japanese traditional food materials. 1

The Imperial Palace has served as the residential place of the successive Emperors since 1868. 2

The Tokyo Skytree (東京スカイツリー) is a television broadcasting tower and landmark of Tokyo. 3

Senso-ji (浅草寺) is Tokyo's oldest temple, completed in 645 AD. It is located in Asakusa, Tokyo. 3

The temple was the first temple built in Asia and the first temple in Japan of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church). 4

Tokyo Tower (東京タワー) is a communications and observation tower in the Shiba-koen district of Minato, Tokyo, Japan. 3

Items to Pack

  • Passport and Visa
  • Comfortable Walking Shoes
  • Camera
  • Train Map
  • Cash or Japanese Card
