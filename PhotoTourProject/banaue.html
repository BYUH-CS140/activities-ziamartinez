Banaue is famous for its spectacular rice terraces, often called the "Eighth Wonder of the World." These terraces were carved into the mountains over 2,000 years ago by the indigenous Ifugao people. Banaue offers a glimpse into traditional Filipino culture amidst stunning natural landscapes.
